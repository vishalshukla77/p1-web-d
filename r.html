PLACEMENTS

At the outset, Indian Institute of Information Technology Ranchi (IIIT Ranchi) is a fully the autonomous technical institute set up by an act of Parliament 2016 as an ‘Institute of National Importance’. It is operational in Public-Private Partnership (PPP) Mode with financial and commitment administrative support of Government of India (50%), Government of Jharkhand (35%) and industry partners - Central Coalfield Limited (CCL) (5%), Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) (5%) and Tata Technologies Ltd (5%). IIIT Ranchi is the only institute dedicated to Information Technology in the state of Jharkhand working in close collaboration with the industry and dedicated to giving IT support to mining and mineral industries as well as higher technical education and skill development in IT. IIIT Ranchi draws on a reserve of extraordinary talent by selective, stringent and amazingly successful Joint Entrance Exam (JEE MAIN) for admission to its UG Programs with an intake of 60 students each in B. Tech (Honours) Computer Science Engineering & B. Tech (Honours) Electronics and Communication Engineering. However, in the first batch admitted in an academic session, the sanctioned student strength was only 60 with 30 in two individual branches. At present, we have a strength of 120 students in a batch with 60 students per branch per batch. NIT Jamshedpur stands as the mentor Institute of IIIT Ranchi and the Institute is currently running from a temporary independent campus at Namkum, Ranchi, allotted by the Government of Jharkhand. The Institute's main campus of 52 acres has been allotted at Kanke, Ranchi and will be functioning by the year 2021.
